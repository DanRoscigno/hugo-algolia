# Sweet Potato Wedges

Serves 4

**Ingredients:**

- 2 medium sweet potatoes 
- ¼ cup olive oil, divided
- ½ teaspoon smoked paprika 
- ½ teaspoon salt 
- ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder 
- ⅛ teaspoon ground cinnamon 
- ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper
