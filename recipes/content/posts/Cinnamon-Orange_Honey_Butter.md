# Cinnamon-Orange Honey Butter

`Condiments`

Makes 3/4 cup

**Ingredients:**

- 1/2 cup butter, softened
- 1/4 cup honey
- 1 to 2 teaspoons orange zest
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

**Directions:**

1. In a small bowl, combine all ingredients. Serve immediately. Refrigerate leftovers.
