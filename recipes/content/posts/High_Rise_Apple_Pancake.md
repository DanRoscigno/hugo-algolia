# High Rise Apple Pancake

`Breakfasts` `Breads`

Servings: 8Â  Â  Â  

**Ingredients:Â  Â  Â** 

1/4 cup butter

6 cups peeled apple slices

1/4 cup sugar

1/2 tsp cinnamon

2 eggs slightly beaten

1/2 cup of all purpose flour

1/2 cup milk

1/4 tsp salt

1 Tbsp butter while heating up cast iron skillet

Topping:

Strawberry preserves

**Directions:**

Preheat oven to 450. Combine sliced apples with cinnamon and sugar. Melt butter in large skillet over low heat. Add apple mixture and cook until apples are tender. Remove from heat. Combine eggs, flour, milk, and salt and beat until smooth. Heat a cast iron (or oven proof skillet--but the cast iron works best) skillet with butter, Immediately pour in the batter. Bake on the lowest rack in oven at 450 degrees for 10 mins. Reduce heat to 350 degrees and continue baking 10 more mins or until golden brown and all puffed up. Fill with apple mixture and top with strawberry preserves if you wish.Â  Â  Â
