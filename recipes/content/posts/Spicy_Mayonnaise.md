# Spicy Mayonnaise

`Condiments`

**Ingredients:**

- 1 cup mayonnaise 
- 1/4 cup Siracha or any chili sauce
- 1/2 Tablespoon lemon juice

**Directions:**

- Combine all the ingredients in a small bowl and whisk until combined.
- Keeps for 1 week.
