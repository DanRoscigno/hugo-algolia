In a large bowl beat 3/4 cup butter at room temp. and 1 cup granulated sugar with electric mixer until fluffy. 

Beat in 1 cup light molasses. With mixer on low gradually add flour mixture and beat just till blended. 

Divid dough in half. Pat each half ito a flattened 1" thick round. Wrap and chill 1 hour or until dough is firm enough to shape. 

Heat oven to 375 degrees. Lightly grease cookie sheets. Form tablespoonfuls dough into 1" balls, roll in remaining 1/4 cup sugar.
