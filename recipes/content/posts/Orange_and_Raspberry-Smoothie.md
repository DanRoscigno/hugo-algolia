# Orange and Raspberry  Smoothie

`Drinks`

Makes 2 ~15 oz drinks or more smaller drinks

**Ingredients:**

11/3 cups raspberries, frozen

1 cup plain Greek yogurt

11/4 cup freshly squeezed orange juice

**Directions:**

Blend on smoothie mode until smooth.
